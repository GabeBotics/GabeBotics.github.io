Algebraic Madness!

Rules:

  • Click start to begin the game
  • You will have 360 seconds to answer as many questions as possible.
  • The questions will appear in the top box.
  • There are 25 questions total.
  • There will be 1 correct answer and 3 incorrect answers.
  • Each correct answer gives you 1 point!
  • Each incorrect answer takes away 10 seconds from your time!
  • Click on the answer box to select an answer.
  • Click reset to reset the game for your next attempt!
  • Get a score of 25 to win the game!
