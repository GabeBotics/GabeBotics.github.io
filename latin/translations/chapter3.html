At first he became attached to these consuls, one of whom was able to provide things for the purpose of the greatest writing, the second of whom was able to provide not only exploits but alao eagerness and ears. Immediately the Luculli, even when Archias was then young, they welcomed him into their family. This family gave not only talent and education, but also nature and virtue, that home, which favors the youngness of the man at first, the same family was very friendly with his old age. At this time he was pleasant to that Metellus the Numidican and his son Pius; he was heard by Marcus Aemilio; he was living with Quintus Catulo, both father and son; He was cultivated by Lucius Crassus. Truly he was holding the Lucullus, Drusus, Octavius, Cato, and the entire house of Hortensia bound by intimacy, he was treated with the highest honor, because not only those who were eager to perceive and hear anything but also if anyone by chance was pretending to hear and perceive were cultivating him
